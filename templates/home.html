Welcome to TechPlan!

What is E-waste?

E-waste is all about the gadgets we love – like smartphones, laptops, and TVs – finding a second life instead of harming our planet. Let's explore how we can make a difference by recycling and responsibly disposing of our electronics, keeping our environment safe and vibrant for future adventures!

Who are we?

We're a group of students from Seattle's University of Washington who are developing a simple, accessible, and seamless tool to both raise awareness around the mechanisms and impacts of electronic waste and to connect people to providers of tools and services to make it easy to use and dispose of technology sustainably.

How to use this site

In the left sidebar, you can find a collection of educational topics to provide you with a fast and digestible introduction to what's going on with "e-waste" and why it matters. Then, explore our searchable index of sustainability resources, curated for several of the major cities in the United States, to easily find what suits your needs.
